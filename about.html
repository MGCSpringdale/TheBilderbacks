Our family

It started around a piano at home. It still does.

The Bilderbacks are a Southern Gospel family ministry from Springdale, Arkansas. For more than 29 years — since 1997 — Euel and Marsha, together with Blake, Quincie, Cortni, and Caleb, have shared the Gospel of Jesus through music, one gathering at a time.

We sing in churches, at revivals, and at festivals — anywhere folks will gather. The songs may change; the heart behind them doesn't. We mean every word.

With four albums to our name — including Full Circle and Our Family Heritage — our music is available on Apple Music, Spotify, and Amazon Music. But the best way to hear us is live. Come find us.
